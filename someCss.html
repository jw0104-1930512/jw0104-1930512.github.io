The Forbidden City

About It

The Forbidden City is a palace complex in central Beijing, China.

It is the largest palace in the world.

houses the Palace Museum,

and was the former Chinese imperial palace and winter residence of the Emperor of China

from the Ming dynasty to the end of the Qing dynasty,

between 1420 and 1924.
